Get to know Mary Claire

Mary Claire is a passionate creative professional whose roots in a tight-knit Appalachian community have shaped her love for culture, education, and the arts. From an early age, she was immersed in experiences that blended creativity with exploration, whether performing onstage, engaging in hands-on science programs, or participating in community-based learning that stretched beyond the classroom.


She believes in the power of honoring tradition while embracing forward-thinking perspectives. Her work is guided by a strong sense of purpose and connection to place, and she continues to elevate stories, communities, and initiatives that embody resilience and innovation.


Mary Claire’s journey reflects a commitment to celebrating the people and places that shape identity while helping build a future grounded in both heritage and possibility.
